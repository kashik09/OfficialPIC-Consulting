About Us

Phantom Impact Consulting (PIC) provides evidence-based solutions for humanitarian and development sectors across Africa and beyond. We specialize in market and social research, policy evaluations, and capacity-building programs, collaborating with governments, NGOs, and international organizations.

Our focus areas include Food Security, WaSH, Disaster Risk Reduction, Gender & Protection, Health & Nutrition, and Peacebuilding. We are committed to strengthening institutions, shaping policies, and driving positive change through innovation and collaboration.

Services

Phantom Impact Consulting (PIC) provides evidence-based solutions in research, policy, strategy, and capacity development.

Market Research

PIC conducts industry trend analysis, market segmentation, consumer behavior research, and SWOT analysis. Our data-driven insights support strategic decision-making and competitive intelligence.

Social Research

Our social research services assess policy impacts, behavioral studies, and social impact assessments. We utilize both quantitative and qualitative methodologies to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Capacity Development & Policy Advisory

PIC strengthens institutions through training programs, technical assistance, and evidence-based policy recommendations to enhance governance and organizational performance.

Program Evaluations & Risk Governance

We conduct third-party monitoring, impact assessments, and performance audits to evaluate program efficiency. Our risk governance strategies enhance resilience and disaster preparedness.
