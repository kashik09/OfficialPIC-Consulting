About Us

Phantom Impact Consulting (PIC) provides evidence-based solutions for humanitarian and development sectors across Africa and beyond. We specialize in market and social research, policy evaluations, and capacity-building programs, collaborating with governments, NGOs, and international organizations.

Our focus areas include Food Security, WaSH, Disaster Risk Reduction, Gender & Protection, Health & Nutrition, and Peacebuilding. We are committed to strengthening institutions, shaping policies, and driving positive change through innovation and collaboration.
